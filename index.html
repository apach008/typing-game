How to Play

Type the word above the closest active threat. The hero handles the move when the word is complete.

Target Priority

Always type the next threat first: the leftmost word is the one about to reach the hero.

Hearts

You start with three hearts. Missed required threats cost one; optional pickups can safely pass by.

Streaks

Clean words build your streak, raise the score multiplier, and trigger combo bonuses.

Powerups

Time adds seconds, Focus slows threats, Shield blocks one mistake, and Magnet boosts pickup value.

Threats

Rocks and bridges make you jump. Ground, flying, shielded, and boss enemies make you attack.

Treasure

Chests and powerups are optional. Type them when you can, but never before an immediate danger.
